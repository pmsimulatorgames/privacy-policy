01. Welcome

Welcome to Hardcore Platformer 2D ("the Game"). This Privacy Policy explains how PM Simulator Games ("we," "us," or "our"), an individual developer based in Hisar, Haryana, India, collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use our mobile game application.

By downloading, installing, or playing the Game, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Game.

02. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We do not directly collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address through the Game itself.

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We collect non-personal information automatically through third-party services integrated into the Game:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (such as Android Advertising ID), mobile network information
  • Game Usage Data: Game events, sessions, progress, levels completed, in-game actions, crash reports, performance data
  • Technical Data: IP address, time zone settings, and general location (country/city level based on IP)
  • Advertising Data: Ad impressions, clicks, ad interaction data, and advertising identifiers

2.3 Permissions We Request

  • Internet Access: Required to display ads, sync game data, and connect to third-party services
  • Phone Notifications: To send you notifications about game updates, events, and promotional offers (you can disable these in your device settings)

03. How We Use Your Information

  • Game Functionality: To provide, maintain, and improve the Game experience
  • Analytics: To understand how users interact with the Game, identify bugs, and improve performance
  • Advertising: To display personalized and non-personalized advertisements via LevelPlay (IronSource) SDK
  • Crash Reporting: To identify and fix technical issues and crashes
  • Communications: To send push notifications about game updates, events, and offers
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

Age Restriction: Hardcore Platformer 2D is intended for general audiences and may be played by children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction) without verifiable parental consent. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without proper consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.
